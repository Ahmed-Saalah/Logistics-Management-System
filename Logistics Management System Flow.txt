1)
- Project Scope:
Project Name: Logistics Management System
•Technologies: C#, ASP.NET Core, SQL Server, Stripe (for payments), Routing Algorithm
•Description: Developed the backend of a logistics management system designed to manage shipments, track
orders, and handle customer payments. The system enables order creation, status tracking, payment
processing, and customer management. It supports multiple shipping methods and integrates with external
payment gateways without requiring route optimization for deliveries.

3) Relations:
Customer and Shipment : (1:M) A customer can have multiple shipments.
Shipment and Payment: (1:1) Each shipment is associated with a single payment record.
Shipment and Shipment Method: (M:1) Multiple shipments can share the same shipping method.

4) Endpoints for the web API:
1- Customer Management: 
. Post/customer : Create Customer
. Get/customer/{id}: Get customer details
. PUT/customer/{id} : Update customer information.
- Delete/customer/{id} : Delete customer.

2- Shipment Management:
. Post/shipment : Create a anew shipment (linking customer, items, and shipping method).
. Get/shipment/{id}: Get details of a specific shipment.
. Put/shipment/{id}: Update an existing shipment.
. Get/shipment: Get a list of shipments (with filters for status, customer, etc).

3- Payment Management:
. Post/payment : Create a new payment for an shipment (integrate with stripe for payment processing)
. Get/payment/{id}: get details of a specific payment
. Get/payment/shipment/{shipemntId} : Get a payment details for a specific shipment.
